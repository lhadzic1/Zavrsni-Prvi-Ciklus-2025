Accessibility & Personalization Settings

Our website includes features that allow you to customize the interface to your needs:

  • Background Colors & Themes - Choose between different color schemes, including muted and high-contrast options.
  • Font Style & Size - Adjust the font family and size for easier readability.
  • Contrast Control - Increase or decrease text contrast.
  • Animation Settings - Enable or disable interface animations to reduce distractions.
  • Voice Controls - Use voice controls to turn on and off out-loud reading.

You can access these options anytime by going to Settings.
